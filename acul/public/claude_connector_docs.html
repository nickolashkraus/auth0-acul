Overview

The Function Health Connector brings your comprehensive health data into Claude, enabling natural conversations about your biomarkers, lab results, and personalized health protocols. Function Health is a preventive health company that tests 100+ biomarkers to provide a complete picture of your health.

With this connector, you can:

View your lab results with at-a-glance summaries of in-range and out-of-range biomarkers
Explore biomarkers by category across 20+ health categories including Heart, Metabolic, Thyroid, and more
Access your nutrition protocol with personalized meal plans and dietary recommendations

All data is retrieved securely from your Function Health account using OAuth authentication.

Available Tools

The Function Health Connector provides three tools for accessing your health data.

Tool 1

Overall Lab Results Summary

Retrieves a high-level summary of your biomarker data with counts of in-range and out-of-range results.

Example Prompts

"Show me my lab results" "How are my biomarkers looking?" "Give me an overview of my health data" "What's my current health status?"

What You'll See

  • Total biomarker count
  • Number of biomarkers in range
  • Number of biomarkers out of range

Parameters (Optional)

Parameter Description Default
categories Filter to specific biomarker categories All categories
showOutOfRangeOnly Only show out-of-range biomarkers false
includeTrends Include longitudinal trend analysis true
Tool 2

Biomarker Category Summary

Provides a breakdown of your biomarkers organized by health category, showing detailed counts for each category.

Example Prompts

"Show my biomarkers by category" "How is my heart health?" "What does my metabolic panel look like?" "Show me my thyroid results" "Break down my health data by category"

What You'll See

  • Category-by-category breakdown
  • In-range and out-of-range counts per category
  • Expandable category cards with details

Parameters (Optional)

Parameter Description Default
categories Filter to specific categories (e.g., ["Heart", "Metabolic"]) All categories
Tool 3

Health Protocol (Nutrition)

Retrieves your personalized health protocol including nutrition goals, dietary recommendations, and custom meal plans.

Example Prompts

"What's my meal plan?" "Show me my nutrition protocol" "What should I be eating to achieve my health goals?" "Give me my dietary recommendations" "What are my health goals?"

What You'll See

  • Daily nutrition goals (calories, protein, carbs, fats)
  • Dietary recommendations tailored to your biomarkers
  • Personalized meal plan with recipes

Parameters (Optional)

Parameter Description Default
health_goals Specific health goals to focus on All goals
action_plan_category Filter to specific protocol type All types
context Additional context for personalization None

Authentication

The Function Health Connector uses OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication.

How It Works

1

When you first connect, you'll be redirected to Function Health to log in

2

You'll authorize Claude to access your health data

3

The connector receives a secure access token

4

This token is automatically refreshed to maintain your session

Required Permissions (Scopes)

When you authorize the connector, you grant the following permissions:

Scope Description
read:health_summary View your overall lab results and biomarker summary
read:biomarkers View detailed biomarker data organized by category
read:action_plan View your health protocols and nutrition plans (action-plans)

Token Security

  • Access tokens are encrypted and securely stored by Claude
  • Tokens automatically refresh before expiration
  • You can revoke access at any time from your Function Health account settings

Data Privacy & Security

Function Health takes your privacy seriously. Here's how we protect your data.

What Data is Accessed

The connector retrieves summary-level data only:

  • Biomarker counts (total, in-range, out-of-range)
  • Category names and aggregate statistics
  • Nutrition goals and dietary recommendations
  • Meal plans and recipes

What Data is NOT Shared

To minimize data exposure, the following are never sent through the connector:

  • Individual biomarker values or specific test results
  • Biomarker IDs or internal identifiers
  • Email addresses or contact information
  • Detailed personal health information beyond nutrition protocols

Security Measures

  • OAuth 2.0 with PKCE for secure authentication
  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Scope-based authorization limiting access to only what's needed
  • Read-only access ‚Äî the connector cannot modify your data
  • RFC 9728 Protected Resource Metadata compliance

Your Control

  • Disconnect the connector at any time from Claude settings
  • Revoke access from your Function Health account
  • Request data deletion per applicable privacy laws

Working Examples

Example 1

Troubleshooting

"Authentication Required" Error

Cause

Your session has expired or the connector needs to be reconnected.

Solution
  1. Go to Claude Settings ‚Üí Connectors
  2. Find Function Health and click Reconnect
  3. Complete the OAuth flow again

No Data Displayed

Cause

Your Function Health account may not have completed lab results yet.

Solution
  1. Log in to your Function Health account directly
  2. Verify that you have received your lab results
  3. If results are pending, wait for them to be processed

"Insufficient Permissions" Error

Cause

The required scopes weren't granted during authorization.

Solution
  1. Disconnect the connector from Claude
  2. Reconnect and ensure you approve all requested permissions
  3. Check that your Function Health account has access to the requested data

Tool Returns Empty Results

Cause

The requested data type may not be available for your account.

Solution
  • For nutrition protocols: Ensure you have a meal plan generated in Function Health
  • For biomarker categories: Verify you have results in the requested category
